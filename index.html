About

I received my PhD from the Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning Laboratory at Hokkaido University in 2021. From 2021 to 2025, I served as a Specially Appointed Assistant Professor in the Database Laboratory at Nagoya University. Since October 2025, I have been a Specially Appointed Assistant Professor at the University of Yamanashi.

Research Interests

  • Locality-Sensitive Hashing
  • Randomized Algorithms
  • Approximate Nearest Neighbor Search
